About Us

RenewPoly is a next-generation, research-driven materials startup accelerating the transition toward sustainable composite solutions. We develop renewable, high-performance furfuryl alcohol–based resin systems designed to replace conventional fossil-based materials in highly demanding applications with stringent fire-resistance requirements.

Our mission is to combine scientific excellence with industrial applicability to deliver materials that meet the highest standards of mechanical performance, thermal stability, and fire safety while prioritizing human health and environmental responsibility.

Our Products

Engineered for highly regulated industries, our furfuryl alcohol–based resins provide intrinsic fire resistance with low Fire, Smoke, and Toxicity (FST) emissions.

• Aerospace • Transportation & Rail • Automotive • Sports & Leisure Equipment • Renewable Energy

  • Drop-in Phenolic-resin alternative
  • Reduced health risks by eliminating toxic phenolic substances
  • Intrinsic fire resistance with low Fire, Smoke and toxicity (FST) emissions
  • Excellent thermal stability and mechanical performance
  • Drop-in compatibility with composite manufacturing processes
  • Strong sustainability impact through renewable raw materials

FlexFuran-B

Resin system engineered for battery and energy storage components, providing intrinsic fire resistance and low FST emissions.

Typical Applications: Battery housings, modules, enclosures, insulation components.

FlexFuran-P

Resin optimized for prepreg manufacturing with excellent fiber wet-out, controlled viscosity, and superior thermal and mechanical properties.

Typical Applications: composites, structural laminates, lightweight panels.

FlexFuran-F

Resin formulation developed for friction material systems requiring thermal stability, wear resistance, and consistent performance under extreme conditions.

Typical Applications: Brake pads, clutch facings, industrial friction components.
